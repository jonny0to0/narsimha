Frequently Asked Questions

Tattoo pricing varies based on size, complexity, and placement. Our minimum is $80, with most pieces ranging from $150-$500. We offer free consultations to provide accurate quotes.
Initial healing takes 2-3 weeks, with complete healing occurring over 2-3 months. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and support throughout the process.
We primarily work by appointment to ensure quality time with each client. However, we do accept walk-ins for consultations and small pieces when artists are available.
