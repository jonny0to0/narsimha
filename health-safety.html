Health & Safety

Your safety and well-being are our top priorities

Our Safety Standards

At Narshimha Tattoo Studio, we maintain the highest standards of health and safety to ensure your well-being and peace of mind.

  • All equipment is sterilized using medical-grade autoclaves
  • Single-use needles and disposable materials for every client
  • Regular health department inspections and certifications
  • Bloodborne pathogen training for all staff members
  • Proper disposal of all biohazardous materials

Pre-Appointment Guidelines

What to Do:

  • Get a good night's sleep
  • Eat a substantial meal 2-3 hours before
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Bring valid photo ID
  • Arrive on time for your appointment

What to Avoid:

  • Alcohol consumption 24 hours before
  • Blood thinners (aspirin, ibuprofen)
  • Excessive caffeine
  • Sun exposure on the tattoo area
  • Tanning beds or spray tans
  • Stressful activities before your session

Medical Considerations

Please inform us if you have any of the following conditions:

Health Conditions:

  • Diabetes or blood sugar issues
  • Heart conditions or pacemaker
  • Blood clotting disorders
  • Autoimmune diseases
  • Skin conditions (eczema, psoriasis)
  • Allergies to metals or inks

Medications:

  • Blood thinners (Warfarin, Heparin)
  • Immunosuppressants
  • Accutane or similar medications
  • Antibiotics (current course)
  • Steroids or cortisone
  • Any medication affecting healing

Aftercare Instructions

First 24 Hours:

  • Keep the bandage on for 2-4 hours
  • Gently wash with antibacterial soap
  • Pat dry with clean paper towel
  • Apply thin layer of recommended ointment
  • Avoid touching the tattoo with dirty hands

First Week:

  • Wash 2-3 times daily with antibacterial soap
  • Apply ointment 2-3 times daily
  • Wear loose, breathable clothing
  • Sleep on clean sheets
  • Avoid swimming, hot tubs, or saunas

What to Avoid:

  • Picking or scratching the tattoo
  • Direct sunlight or tanning
  • Heavy exercise for first few days
  • Submerging in water (baths, pools)
  • Using harsh soaps or scrubs

Warning Signs - Contact Us Immediately

If you experience any of the following, contact us immediately:

  • Excessive redness, swelling, or heat around the tattoo
  • Pus or unusual discharge
  • Fever or chills
  • Rash or allergic reaction
  • Severe pain that doesn't improve
  • Any concerns about healing

Studio Cleanliness

We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and sterilization:

  • All surfaces disinfected between clients
  • Sterilized equipment for each procedure
  • Disposable barriers on all equipment
  • Regular health department inspections
  • Proper waste disposal procedures
  • Clean, well-ventilated workspace
